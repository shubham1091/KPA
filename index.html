Who I Am

Hi, I'm Shubham! As a Master's student in Business Analytics at Keele University, I understand the unique challenges we face as postgraduates.

Like you, I balance academics, work, and the search for community in a demanding environment. Whether you've seen me studying in the library, chatting in campus cafés ☕, or attending KPA events, you know I'm approachable and genuinely invested in our postgraduate community.

The KPA transformed my Keele experience by providing a welcoming community when I needed it most. Now, I want to give back by serving as your Secretary – not for the title, but to ensure every postgraduate student feels supported, heard, and valued.

My Qualifications

Lived Experience

As an international student, I've navigated academic pressure, financial concerns, and adaptation to a new environment. I understand firsthand the challenges you face.

Professional Background

My experience in education, customer service, and community leadership has equipped me with strong organizational and communication skills essential for the Secretary role.

Inclusive Perspective

I understand the diverse needs of home students, international students, full-time and part-time learners. Everyone deserves representation in the KPA.

My Action Plan

1 Clearer Access to Real Help

  • Create simple, visible guides to academic, wellbeing, funding, and career support.
  • Run monthly “Know Your Services” posts and workshops to demystify support systems.
  • Make help feel closer, quicker, and less overwhelming for everyone.

2 Community Without Pressure

  • Organise relaxed activities like Study Cafés, Coffee Hours, and Wellbeing Walks.
  • Encourage social connection without forcing interaction — zero pressure, all welcome.
  • Celebrate culture and diversity through small, inclusive events.

3 Real Listening, Real Action

  • Use micro-surveys, suggestion boxes, and termly forums to hear your voice.
  • Publish “You Said, We Did” updates so you see real change from your feedback.
  • Represent all student groups equally — full-time, part-time, international, home.

4 Focus on Mental Health & Wellbeing

  • Work with university services to raise awareness of mental health resources.
  • Promote peer check-ins, wellbeing walks, and moments to pause and breathe.
  • Make mental health support visible, approachable, and stigma-free.

5 Encourage Peer Support Programmes

  • Support mentorship schemes for postgraduates at every stage.
  • Promote study groups, skill swaps, and international student buddy initiatives.
  • Help build networks where students can support and uplift each other.

6 Strengthen Awareness of Student Reps

  • Help students understand the role of their course and school representatives.
  • Work to increase visibility and approachability of KPA and school reps alike.
  • Make it easier for students to know who to go to when they need help or change.

My Commitment

Postgraduate life presents unique challenges - your KPA should make navigating them easier. I'm committed to building a KPA that truly serves you through attentive listening, decisive action, and genuine care.

With your support, I'll work tirelessly to create a community where every student feels they truly belong.
